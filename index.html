Evereone Can Draw

I read that about two years ago

So, I decided to prove them wrong and gave it a shot. I was 64 at the time and I couldn't even draw a stick figure so I knew it would be a challenge. I signed up for some art classes on Udemy.com and started the journey. I started off with pencil drawing and some art classes for computer design and I'm currently doing some color pencil courses. I found it important to not trace a design but to actually jump in and draw. To my surprise I actually could draw and I am having a great time learning something that I thought I could never do.

My Art Journey

Art by Steve

As I said earlier this has been a two-year journey. Most of the art that I am posting is the result of classes I have taken along the way. I have trouble drawing from imagination so I use a lot of references. However, nothing is traced.

I was lucky

Early in my journey I was lucky to find a great instruction site call Udemy.com. They have hundreds of instructors teaching any art style you could desire. My current instructor Ava who teaches Masterclass Drawing, Design & Creativity with color pencil, and many others, is a wealth of knowledge. She has packed her course with so many projects that I want to come back every day to see what else I can learn.
