My Color Pencil Art

It's All About Shapes

Rectangles, cylinders, circles, triangles...If you can see the shapes that make up the thing you wish to draw you are 70% of the way to putting it on paper. I never thought that by connecting the shapes and coloring them you could make beautiful art. Of course, beauty is in the eye of the beholder. I'm not saying it is simple but if you put in the time, you can make art. I love my art and thanks to Ava and all my instructors at Udmey.com I have done something that I never thought I could do.
